How It Works
  • The schedule is managed by Celery Beat, which runs as a background service.
  • After changing the schedule, the Celery Beat worker needs to be restarted to pick up the new timing.
  • Saved settings are stored in the database and applied when Celery Beat starts.
  • The Manual Sync button triggers a sync immediately, regardless of the schedule.
